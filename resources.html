2. Property & Natural Resources

Instituto Socioambiental (ISA)

Focuses on indigenous rights (land rights included) and protecting the ecological structure of Brazil.

Visit Site
FICA (Fundo Imobiliário Comunitário para Aluguel)

FICA offers alternative housing options to Favelas. They offer residents real addresses and contractual agreements that allow residents to retain more rights compared to traditionally exploitive contracts.

Visit Site
Students For Liberty: The Favelas Project

Not a charity, but an example of how an organization in the past successfully helped Brazillians by providing the resources and education needed to obtain land titles for hundred of Brazillian families

Visit Site

3. Finance & Technology

EduMais

Provides education for programming, digital literacy, English, and videogame creation. By providing technological centered education, EduMais is preparing young Brazillians to better navigate the online world with relevant skills.

Visit Site
Sitawi

Sitawi acts as a middleman between investors and organizations that are trying to help Brazil. They follow an "impact investment model" where they fund organizations making the most difference. They also provide consultancy and management services for NGOs. They donate to tech, education, and equity.

Visit Site

4. Microfinance

Sebrae

Offers guidance, training, networking, and funding to Brazillian entrepreneurs. Helping small businesses get off the ground diversifies Brazils economy and creates more efficient markets.
